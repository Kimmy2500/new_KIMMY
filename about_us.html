ABOUT US

"Le petit Flower" wants to share beautiful flowers with everyone.

BRAND STORY

"Le petit Flower" is run by a 10-year-old florist "Eva". Our brand is pre-sold to sell flowers at reasonable prices. Online delivery is not available and can only be booked. If you book flowers online and by phone, you can come to the store and pick up the day you specified. The "EVENT" page once every two months allows you to see a variety of flowers at lower prices.
